Source 2 - Stroads are Ugly, Expensive, and Dangerous (and they're everywhere) [ST05]

This is a video made by a channel that's dedicated to discussing human-centric city-planning. This video describe "strode" as a combination between roads and streets. Roads is designed for cars and is the space/mean to get from point A to point B while streets are more of a destination and more for human. Strode is a combination of both which isn't great by many aspects such as not being human-friendly and also dangerous for all parties involved. This video act as is a great example to my point for my project.
